Hello, Trudeau?

Wesley Ou
December 9, 2021

We all know Justin Trudeau is no stranger to breaking electoral promises. From supplying clean water to Indigenous communities, to budget deficits, his string of mishaps seems to never end. Back during the 2015 election campaign, he promised to abolish the first-past-the-post system, but of course, two elections later, still no change has occurred. This is a concurrent example of how the Liberal government continually fails Canadians every step of the way. Research conducted by the Angus Reid institute finds that three in five Canadians would have preferred an election using the proportional representation model. Not only has Trudeau deterred his reform plans, he also has completely switched sides on the issue. During the 2021 campaign, he told media that he would not favor proportional representation as the electoral system, and instead was looking into a ranked ballot. As always, Trudeau decided to decept Canadians and voters alike with his false claims.
