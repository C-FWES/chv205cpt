Reform Now

Wesley Ou
December 9, 2021

Let’s face it. The current electoral system is flawed. Canadian citizens are being denied their fair share of representation in our first-past-the-post system. We are being squashed into voting for who we think will win instead of freely voicing our opinions. Now, you might be wondering, what can we do to ensure fair representation for all? The answer is as simple as it gets - proportional representation. This means that everyone, no matter where they live, will be able to contribute their opinion to form a well constructed government. The new reform would abolish several issues, including finally respecting the popular vote. In the 2021 election, the Conservative party received 33.7% of the popular vote, yet the Liberals amassed a victory with only 32.6% of the vote. This is in no way ethical or fair to Canadian voters, and it is time that we come together to push for reform. Our votes are what define our country - let’s make them count.
