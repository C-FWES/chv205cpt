ABOUT US

Founded in 2021, the Canadian Electoral Initiative strives to be a leading interest group for electoral reform and the implementation of a fair proportional representation system. We lobby for just representation in the Parliament and in other levels of government. We also push in interest for fair representation of voters and to make every single vote count. This means equivalent representation for all of our citizens, no matter rural or urban.
